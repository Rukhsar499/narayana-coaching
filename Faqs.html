FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the eKidz programme offered by Narayana Shikshayatan?

The eKidz programme is an early childhood education programme that cultivates core skills in a nurturing environment. It builds confidence, social skills, and communication skills with a limited student-teacher ratio. The programme provides a secure, clean, and stimulating environment for young minds to grow.

In what way does the eChamps programme contribute to a child's growth?

The eChamps programme aims for development as a whole through development of communication skills, emotional quotient, and interpersonal relations. It employs activity-based learning to create conceptual understanding and utilises personalised guidance through the Learner Support Program and Adoption Program.

Why is the eTechno programme essential for students of Classes VI to X?

The eTechno programme equips students for a competitive world by combining intellectual rigour and emotional well-being. It adopts a systematic way with the 4Cs approach—Common Teaching, Common Micro-Schedule, Common Examination, and Common Analysis—guaranteeing regular and effective learning. It also adds Olympiads and competitive exams to enhance confidence.

Why should I take senior secondary education in Narayana Shikshayatan?

Narayana Shikshayatan has a well-developed senior secondary curriculum that balances academic brilliance and competitive exam preparation. We impart professional coaching for IIT-JEE, NEET, CA-CPT, and UPSC along with developing life skills such as communication, time management, and emotional stability to prepare students for exams as well as the challenges of life.

Are transport facilities available for my ward?

Yes, you can avail yourself of the transport facilities for your child. Our drivers and transport staff members are well-trained to handle any challenging situation. Moreover, we prioritise the safety of our schoolchildren more than anything else.

Can you guarantee the safety of my child inside the laboratories?

Yes, we can assure you about your child’s safety not just inside the different laboratories but also anywhere inside the school premises. To date, there has been no report of mishap or negligence as such. Our lab assistants, as well as the teaching and non-teaching staff members of the school, are well-trained to provide the utmost safety for each child.

About Us

Another feather in the cap of the well-known Narayana Group is Narayana Shikshayatan. We are a prime illustration of West Bengal's superior educational system. With a distinguished history spanning over 40 years, it offers a supportive environment for children to learn, develop, and explore.
