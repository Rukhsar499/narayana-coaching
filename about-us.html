About Narayana

We, at Narayana Shikshayatan, believe that every child can become a champion if provided with the proper kind of guidance, values, and care. As a proud affiliate of the West Bengal Board, where the medium of instruction is English, we offer a perfect blend of academic excellence and character formation. Our experienced teachers, modern teaching methods, and holistic approach to education prepare the students to be self-assured, compassionate, and accountable individuals. Encompassing scholastic as well as co-scholastic development, we aim to create an atmosphere of happiness where every child would be nurtured, encouraged, and empowered to reach their dreams. This is where the futures take shape.

Our Mission

With steady conviction, we envision a future where each learner is intentionally growing, wholeheartedly embracing growth, and confidently giving back to their communities with love. Our vision will nurture imaginations that dream big while giving back with heart.

Our Vision

At Narayana Shikshayatan, we have the goal of developing students who excel academically but also foster a love of health competition. It encourages students to pursue their goals with confidence, persistence, and honour and helps them realise their fullest potential for true success.

Our Journey

  • Narayana Shikshayatan started with a genuine vision of fostering young minds with academic brilliance and future leaders with solid values. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in education across West Bengal, inspiring thousands of students to dream bigger and aim higher.
  • Guided by our vision of determination, progress, and service, we’ve created an environment where learning is joyful, disciplined, and meaningful. From world-class infrastructure and innovative pedagogy to committed teachers and a competitive environment, every step we take is towards all-round development. We believe in preparing students exam-ready as well as life-ready.
  • As we evolve, our commitment continues to be the same: to empower each child to discover their real potential, pursue their dreams, and rise with confidence. This journey is not just education—it's a promise to build a better tomorrow.
