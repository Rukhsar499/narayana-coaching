E-Techno

Welcome to Narayana Shikshayatan School's eTechno Programme: Enabling Young Minds for a Vibrant Future

We at Narayana Shikshayatan School firmly believe that middle and secondary school years (VI to X) are the most critical years in fashioning the academic career and development of a student. To meet the changing education scenario and aspirations of our students, we have the pleasure in launching the eTechno Programme—a holistic education program to incorporate excellence in academics with overall development.

Why the eTechno Programme?

Integrated Curriculum for Concept Mastery

Our program integrates the very best from all the big educational boards for a sound, integrated foundation in basic subjects.

The integrated approach brings about higher-order thinking and problem-solving ability to prepare our students to excel in a very wide range of academic and real-life situations.

The 4Cs Approach: Pillar of Consistency

The core of our program is the 4Cs Approach

Common Teaching

We maintain a common teaching method in all our schools, where we mix theoretical study with practical application. This commonality ensures that every student is provided with an outstanding education tailored to ensure good learning.

Common Micro-Schedule

Our well-thought-out syllabus is dispersed on a monthly and daily schedule throughout the country. This activity breakdown on a daily basis keeps students organised, on track, and in tune with their learning goals.

Common Examination

We administer diversified tests, e.g., models compatible with competitive exams such as JEE and NEET, and Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE)-directed projects. This consistency in all schools ensures a homogenous evaluation system.

Common Analysis

We offer detailed performance reports and analysis in three days of exams. Early feedback ensures a competitive attitude and enables interventions in time to assist student development.

Adoption Calling Programme:

Individualised Mentorship

Knowing that every student has his or her own learning requirements, our Adoption Calling Programme provides individual mentors to offer personalised academic guidance. These mentors are in touch with parents to track student progress, solve issues, and customise learning strategies so that all students are able to achieve their full potential.

Individualised Error Analysis and Correction

To encourage ongoing improvement, we prepare individual error lists for every student following tests. These comprehensive reports classify errors by subject and question, providing feedback that informs focused learning and development.

DISHA Mental Wellness Programme: Enabling Comprehensive Well-being

We believe education to be beyond academics. DISHA Programme focuses on overall well-being, ensuring plenty of mental and emotional support for students, parents, and teachers alike. It reasserts our commitment to creating well-rounded individuals.

Olympiad Programme: Nurturing Competitive Excellence

Our Olympiad Programme is designed for students who aspire to perform at the highest levels in highly competitive settings. Through practical application and the development of critical thinking, we train students to excel at international platforms on various Olympiads and competitions.

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: Enabling Modern Learning

Our vibrant and dynamic learning ecosystem boasts state-of-the-art facilities to support the learning process:

Computer Lab: Offers direct experience with computers, enabling students to become digitally literate and technically skilled.

Robotics Lab: Fosters innovation and direct experience with robotics, helping students prepare for the future development of technology.

Science Lab: Provides hands-on learning experiences through which students are able to conduct experiments and reinforce their knowledge about scientific principles.

Beyond Academics: Holistic Development

National Space Society (NSS) Space Settlement Contest: The contest, administered by NASA, invites students to create innovative solutions to settling space by humans, to access its enormous resources for the benefit of civilisation.

Co-scholastic Activities: Our students engage in a range of varied activities through clubs that foster interdisciplinarity and connect book learning with reality.

Extracurricular Competitions: Extracurricular competitions and activities at our school build global awareness, diplomatic connections, and confident public speaking, equipping students to be future leaders.

nSports: Our physical education program builds mental and physical resilience, sporting excellence, teamwork, and fair play.

Preparing Students for a Competitive World
