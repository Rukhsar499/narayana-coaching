Classroom

Classrooms are essential to learning, according to Narayana Shikshayatan. In light of this, we uphold a friendly atmosphere at all Narayana School locations in West Bengal, where kids are at ease discussing their opinions with their peers and asking teachers any questions they may have.

Moreover, we have established stringent cleaning policies that contribute to maintaining classroom hygiene and establishing a completely secure learning environment. Students are encouraged to maintain their discipline by these actions. In addition, we have air conditioning, cosy furnishings, well-designed interiors, and other amenities that are necessary for students to learn without interruption.

The digital classrooms offered by the Narayana Group of Schools are a notable feature. There are large screens, top-notch projectors, sound systems, and other devices in our digital classrooms.

As one of West Bengal's top WBBSE and WBCHSE English-medium schools, we offer incredibly captivating digital content designed especially for students' self-paced learning. Worksheets, tests, glossaries, 3D animated films, more than 6,000 audio-visual materials, and much more are all included in our digital library. They guarantee that students grasp concepts with ease and encourage interactive learning sessions.

Transport

Ensure Hassle-Free Transportation for Your Child With Our Transport Facilities

Make sure your child has a hassle-free commute with our transport services. Transport to school must be both comfortable and safe, particularly after the pandemic. In Belghoria, Kolkata, Narayana Shikshayatan provides a safe and hygienic transport service that guarantees students are picked up and dropped off at their homes on time.

Well-Maintained Vehicles

We adhere to the highest safety standards at every applicable point of our campus. This is especially true when it comes to the fleet of buses that pick up and drop students to and from school. Our buses go through regular services and routine maintenance to stay in top shape for performance and safety needs.

Trained Staff and Drivers

All our drivers are well-trained, closely follow traffic laws, and drive at a safe speed to ensure a comfortable ride. They also drive on the road with appropriate discipline. These certified drivers have years of expertise in their positions and put children's safety first.

Pick-up & Drop-Off at Door

Narayana Shikshayatan, one of West Bengal's best WBBSE and WBCHSE schools, offers prompt pickup and drop-off services. Parents may rest easy with our door-to-door pickup and drop-off services. Additionally, students save time by not having to deal with the inconvenience of making the extra trip home from their drop-off location.

In addition to our drivers, we also have committed staff members working on the bus to maintain a safe and secure atmosphere so parents can feel secure using our transportation services. In addition to upholding discipline, they notify parents of any changes to the route, time adjustments, etc.

Safety & Security

Safety and Security at Narayana Shikshayatan

At Narayana Shikshayatan, we make sure that students, teachers, and school employees are properly protected and secure. Students' social and creative learning is also supported and encouraged when the learning environment is kept safe.

Additionally, our school works to ensure communication, raise awareness, develop strategies for disaster management, conduct mock drills, and more. Our goal is to secure the school's property and transportation infrastructure to ensure students' safety at all times and to expedite the healing process following any mishap.

Physical Security

School buildings, playgrounds, libraries, science and computer labs, swimming pools, restrooms, school buses, drinking water facilities, and the surrounding regions of school grounds are all kept extremely safe. Our school building, which is among the best WBBSE schools in West Bengal, has fire safety features and earthquake protection to provide complete protection against natural calamities.

Visual Security

To make sure that your child's everyday activities at school are periodically seen and documented, we have placed CCTV cameras in various locations across the school, campus, playground, etc. These cameras serve as a crucial part of security in addition to giving parents peace of mind.

Our Benefits

Regularly cleaning and sanitising the building, playground, library, etc.

Offer immediate medical assistance to children if anyone becomes ill or is injured.
