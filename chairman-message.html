Our Founder

At Narayana Shikshayatan, we share the conviction that education is not just the passing on of information—it's the awakening of the mind, the cultivating of the heart, and the moulding of character. Our vision was conceived from a simple, yet powerful belief: every child should have an education that enables them to dream without fear and achieve with purpose.

Being affiliated with the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education (WBBSE) and having English as a medium of instruction, Narayana Shikshayatan is committed to providing academic excellence combined with a human touch. We have created an atmosphere where discipline and creativity go hand in hand, competition and compassion work in tandem, and learning is joyful.

With more than four decades of experience all over India through the Narayana Group, we are proud to introduce that same dedication, discipline, and dynamism to the students of West Bengal. As we embark on this journey, our vision remains the same—to equip our students not only for exams but for life. I invite each parent and learner to become a part of our expanding family and take a step towards a brighter future full of promise and purpose.

My heartiest welcome to all our newly joined students in Narayana Shikshayatan – you are our future, you are our pride.

About Us

Another feather in the cap of the well-known Narayana Group is Narayana Shikshayatan. We are a prime illustration of West Bengal's superior educational system. With a distinguished history spanning over 40 years, it offers a supportive environment for children to learn, develop, and explore.
