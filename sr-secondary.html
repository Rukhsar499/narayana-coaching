Sr. Secondary

Narayana Shikshayatan: Shaping Lives to Lead a Brighter Future

At Narayana Shikshayatan, we recognise the significance of senior secondary years in defining the scholarly and professional career of a student. Through our affiliation with the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education (WBCHE), our school endeavours to give a holistic education that is more than what is taught through textbooks. While emphasising academic achievement, we also train students for different competitive exams and equip them with the competencies necessary to thrive in today's world.

Holistic Development Approach

Our curriculum is designed with careful planning to enable the growth of each student in all possible ways. Not only does it aim to impart subject matter, but it also enables the growth of life skills like analytical thinking, problem-solving capacity, time management, and emotional stability. We place heavy emphasis on the acquisition of efficient communication skills, leadership skills and self-discipline.

At Narayana Shikshayatan, we envision an environment where students are motivated to discover their highest potential, not only in the classroom but also in life.

Competitive Exam Expert Coaching

Competitive exams are a significant part of the career life of students. Hence, we provide special coaching courses to assist students in achieving success in competitive exams as well as their standard academic courses.

IIT-JEE/NEET Preparation

We offer guidance from experienced faculties to students who are looking to crack the IIT-JEE and NEET entrance exams. These exams are a doorway to some of the best engineering and medical colleges. Since we are associated with Narayana Coaching Centers, which have a legacy of excellence, we offer quality methods of preparation based on the suitability of each student.

CA-CPT & UPSC Coaching

The school also offers expert coaching to those students who are interested in taking the CA-CPT and UPSC exams. We emphasise developing a strong foundation of knowledge and enhancing the reasoning, analytical, and writing skills of the students so that they are apt for these extremely competitive exams.

Attention WBCHE Students!

Prepare for IIT-JEE/NEET, CA-CPT, and UPSC with the best guidance and individualised coaching. Call us today to begin your success story! Our coaching experts will guide you to your dreams.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

At Narayana Shikshayatan, it is our mission to offer the best infrastructure to foster the scholarly and extracurricular growth of our students. Our campus boasts cutting-edge facilities, such as well-equipped libraries, state-of-the-art science and computer labs, and spacious classrooms conducive to interactive learning. We also have large sports facilities to encourage physical fitness and friendship.

Every corner of our campus is set up to challenge and encourage students, with a clean, safe, and challenging environment to grow and discover their talents.
