E-Champs

Where Young Minds Grow in Happiness, Confidence & Curiosity

At Narayana Shikshayatan, we don't think that school life in the early years is all about reading books—it's about learning the world, acquiring good values, and learning to think, communicate, and grow. That's why we have designed the eChamps Programme, a special academic program for Classes I to V, which is particularly designed to bring up children in a happy, systematic, and richly rewarding environment.

Why E-Kidz?

Holistic Growth

We don't instruct—we develop. E-Kidz works on your child's development holistically: mind, heart, social interaction, and body. From counting, turning toys to share, feeling and voicing out emotions, to running through rainbow-coloured hoops in the play area—every experience moulds them well.

Learning Through Play

Kids learn best when they're enjoying themselves. For that reason, our curriculum is rich in stories, games, music, art, dance, and nature walks. Every activity is thoughtfully designed to spark curiosity, inspire creativity, and provide fun learning experiences.

A Safe and Loving Space

Safety is our prime concern. Our classrooms are designed to be cosy, inviting, and child-friendly. Under CCTV surveillance, expert care, and regular health and hygiene checks, parents can have the reassurance that their little ones are being cared for with love.

English Medium Foundation

Being a school of WBBSE medium in English, we establish a strong foundation in communication from the very beginning. The kids are increasingly familiar with speaking English using rhymes, narration, and conversation, thus giving them confidence in reading as well as in speaking.

Building the Individuality of Every Child

Each child is unique. We believe in individualised care at E-Kidz. With our low student-to-teacher ratio, we get to know each child's strengths and weaknesses and let them shine their way.

High Parent Involvement

We understand that children thrive where parents and teachers cooperate. E-Kidz fosters supportive partnerships with families by keeping you regularly updated with reports, parent-teacher meetings, workshops, and play sessions where the learning process involves you quite deeply.

E-Kidz Programs

We have an extensively designed and sequential early childhood program that advances along with your child:

Nursery (2.5–3.5 years)

A happy induction to the world of learning. Kids discover the world around them, start shaping basic motor and social skills, and get introduced to identifying letters, numbers, shapes, and sounds.

LKG (Age 3.5–4.5 years)

With a foundation of the nursery, children begin to learn through guided play. Pre-writing, basic math, primary science, and creative arts take a place in their schedule.

UKG(Age 4.5–5.5 years)

This stage lays the groundwork for school learning. Phonics, sentence construction, story sequencing, number awareness, and overall awareness are taught engagingly and interactively.
