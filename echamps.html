E-Champs

Where Young Minds Grow in Happiness, Confidence & Curiosity

At Narayana Shikshayatan, we don't think that school life in the early years is all about reading books—it's about learning the world, acquiring good values, and learning to think, communicate, and grow. That's why we have designed the eChamps Programme, a special academic program for Classes I to V, which is particularly designed to bring up children in a happy, systematic, and richly rewarding environment.

What is eChamps?

eChamps means Excellence Champions—a core program that inspires kids to be active learners, imaginative thinkers, and caring human beings. With an equal emphasis on academics, life skills, imagination, and values, the program instils overall growth in young minds.

Our Core Objectives

  • Create Conceptual Clarity at an early age
  • Create Curiosity through right and age-sensitive learning
  • Create Communication, Social & Emotional Skills
  • Create Active Engagement, not performance
  • Build Discipline, Creativity, and Confidence

What Makes eChamps Special?

Holistic Learning Plans

We at Narayana Shikshayatan are convinced that true learning reaches the heart and mind simultaneously. That's why our curriculum is carefully designed to provide a perfect blend of English, Math, Science, Social Studies, Value Education, Arts, and Life Skills.

Personalised Learner Support

We know that every child is unique. Some pick up fast, some are slow—and both are absolutely fine. Through vigilant observation, teacher observations, and frequent interaction, we provide just the right amount of encouragement—be it a gentle prod or a pat on the back.

Intelligent Assessments

We think learning should be measured kindly, not graded sternly. That's why our testing process is carefully crafted to foster progress—rather than pressure. Rather than emphasizing only scores, we consider how a child is developing in comprehension, self-assurance, and imagination.

Structured But Happy

Yes, we do support discipline and a carefully orchestrated day—but never at the expense of fun. Our classrooms operate on a routine that provides children with a sense of security, but the modes of teaching are flexible, interactive, and fun.

Skilled & Caring Educators

Behind each confident student lies a teacher who trusts them. At Narayana Shikshayatan, our teachers are not mere instructors—they are mentors, motivators, and gentle guides. We ensure that each child feels valued and nurtured. They do not just teach subjects—they mold personalities.

The Learning Environment

We've thoughtfully planned each nook and cranny of our school to embody warmth, creativity, and security. Our classrooms are cheerful, bright, and welcoming—a delightful blend of comfort and functionality. With digital whiteboards, visual learning tools, colourful displays, and cosy reading nooks, we ensure that the environment sparks curiosity.

Partnering with Parents

We are aware that the first teachers of a child are parents. Thus, we include you at each stage of your child's life through:

1. Regular PTMs and student progress reports

2. Workshop and open-house sessions

3. Interaction

4. Regular communication through our school app and newsletters

Beyond the Classroom

The eChamps Programme extends beyond the textbook. With clubs, competitions, community service, and value-based lessons, we enable children to become:

1. Empathetic and socially responsible

2. Confident communicators

3. Curious learners and lifelong explorers

Curriculum Approach

Our instruction is based on activity-based and experiential learning:

Classes I and II

Activity-based learning using games, storytelling, puzzles, and classroom exercises.

Classes III to V

Transition to experiential learning with projects, presentations, science models, and real-life problem-solving.

Ready to Raise a Champion?

The eChamps Programme at Narayana Shikshayatan is more than an academic foundation—it's a breathtaking journey of self-discovery, growth, and happiness. It's where your child will not only learn lessons but also learn how to live with purpose and confidence.

Come to Narayana Shikshayatan today and taste the difference in eChamps.
